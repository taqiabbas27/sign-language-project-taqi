Welcome to Hands That Speak!

Hands That Speak is an interactive platform designed to make learning American Sign Language (ASL) simple and engaging. This rich visual language uses hand signs, facial expressions, and body language to communicate, promoting inclusivity and enhancing communication with diverse groups. Join us on this journey to learn ASL in a fun and accessible way!

Tips to Learn Sign Language Effectively

  • Practice daily with flashcards or visual aids to reinforce memory.
  • Watch videos or live signers to understand natural flow and expression.
  • Join sign language groups or clubs to practice with others.
  • Be patient and consistent — language learning takes time and effort.
  • Attend local Deaf community events to practice immersive communication.
